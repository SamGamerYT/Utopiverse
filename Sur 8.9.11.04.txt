**Patch/Changes 8.9.11.04** *(Network Changes)*  
> :shiny: [Survival] Added **Easter Eggs** around the **new Spawn**, along with a collection progress hologram.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Fixed the **Epic Crate** not rewarding **Removal Scrolls**.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Updated the **Blackmarket** in **"/shop"** — you no longer need to visit a separate location.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Re-enabled **item dropping** at **"/spawn"**. Abuse of this feature will be dealt with severely.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Introducing the **Custom Resource World**, featuring new structures and puzzles for players to discover.  
> :shiny: [Survival] The **Courier NPC** will now return to his stall near the **Crates Zone** during the night.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Added the **Whippy & SmartName** Valentine pet to roam around the **Pub**.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Added **Doran Stonepath** and **Flora Briarwood**, who roam Spawn and trade selected items for **Emeralds**.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Removed the **Volcano Codex** requirement from **Custom Crop Seeds**.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Removed **"/warp bs"**, **"/warp christ"**, **"/warp christmas"**, and **"/warp upgrade"**.  
> 
> :shiny: [Survival] Added **Ronan Trailward** at **"/warp dungeons"**, providing important guidance for new players.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Fixed **Starter Milestones** still counting data from the previous **Easter Eggs** event.  
> :shiny: [Survival] Added the **Cemetery Zone** (formerly **Graveyard Zone**). The area has been relocated, expanded with more mobs, and now includes a teleportation NPC at **"/warp dungeons"**.
> :shiny: [Survival] Removed **Crafting time** from **Enchanted Scute, Refined Scute, Enchanted Magma & Refined Magma**.
> :shiny: [Survival] **Parkour** has gotten an update with more options for parkour.
> :shiny: [Survival] Reduced **Queen's Seal Ring** requirement from **agility 18** to **14**, **Fighting 24** to **18**.
> :shiny: [Survival] Reduced **Orchid Mantis Cloak** requirement from **Fighting 25** to **18**.
